Meet the Team

Eric Youngson

Eric is ambitious and geared towards engineering and architecture. He currently is the Founder & CEO of Succession Ecological Services which permanently reduces the environmental impact of the built environment. Eric has worked on a total of (85) building analysis projects including new construction projects ranging from a water treatment plant in Washington State, and a science facility at the University of Hawaii to the US Standard Embassy Design. In his free time you can find him outside, either surfing some waves or skiing snowy slopes.

Rob Redding

Rob is a masters student in the multimedia journalism program the University of Oregon. His career interests include film and short documentary. He is originally from Philadelphia and has written for the Baltimore Sun. His main contribution to PlotPDX is writing and journalistic perspectives. Rob authored a nonfiction account about his junior year of college.

Emily Greene

Emily is a student in the professional masters program in journalism at the University of Oregon. Her interests surround multimedia that focuses on the human storytelling. Currently, Emily is an intern at BUCK creative media in Portland and KLCC, a NPR affiliate in Eugene. Due to her travels in Latin America, Emily enjoys a good street taco and a conversation in Spanish.

Joy Alise Davis

Joy is passionate about fostering community dialogue about development. She is the founding partner and principal for Design + Culture Lab, LLC a research-driven urban social enterprise dedicated to the positive transformation of urban neighborhoods. Design + Culture Lab was the Winner of the 2014 Startup PDX Challenge. Joy works hard to keep current and relevant perspectives in mind for PlotPDX. Her current obsessions include Solange Knowles and pizza.

Nikolas Wise

Nikolas is a designer and front end developer. He drives PlotPDX’s technology, from website development to mapping infrastructure. He's been working in design and web development for nearly a decade, and currently is involved with Research and Development for the GIS company Esri. Nikolas is a Portland native, he spends his free time printmaking.

Leslie Waara

Leslie is a multi-disciplinary experience designer. She works on PlotPDX’s website design, structure and branding. She is currently a digital designer at Wieden + Kennedy, a storytelling based ad agency in Portland. She has worked in retail design, website design, and PR. In her free time she is an amateur urban farmer. Her dream is to turn her yard into a self-sustaining food source.

Steve Leathers

Steve is the nicest neighbor you’ll ever have. For the past four years, he has worked as a producer in the city planning consulting world, which mostly consists of making maps. He also works with other graphics and web projects. He started learning HTML when he was 13, so his main role with PlotPDX is the website design and mapping. Steve is an avid dog lover and makes a mean BBQ.
